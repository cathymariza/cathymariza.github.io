  • Choose Song Title and Structure

    • The first step in writing a song is chosing what the Title of the song will be. You can do this by brainstorming different titles and chosing the catchiest of them all.

    After this, you proceed with chossinf a good structure fir your song. You chose the Verse/Chorus/Bridge order that suits your song, and also depending on how long or short it would be

  • Write from Experience or Fantasy

    • Most of the best songs are those whose author is telling a story from within or at least fantasy. Something that people can easily relate to. So, one of the best tips, is to write from either of the two places.

  • Contruct a Temporary Chorus and Verse

    • The next step is constructing what I would call the body parts of the song; and these are none other than the chorus and verse. These contain the actual message that the author wants to share with the world.

    Therefore, the author should take a good amount of time to prepare these thoroughly.

  • Find the Melody in your lyric

    • Lat but not least, the author should of course chose a nice melody that reveals their creativivty and suits the message they want to pass on to the world

    It's a very important step in creating a song.
